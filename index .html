Who I Am

Building knowledge at the frontier

I am an Adjunct Instructor and Researcher at Pacific States University in Los Angeles, California, where I teach programming and UI/UX design to the next generation of STEM professionals.

My research sits at the crossroads of artificial intelligence, federated learning, and blockchain security. I co-author with PSU-affiliated colleagues and target rigorous IEEE publication standards, bringing reproducible, real-world impact to every study.

I am also an active software developer — currently building Legacy Vault (a React Native digital estate planner) and Desh-TV ENPS (a Next.js newsroom management platform).
